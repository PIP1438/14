About Us

Billy - Buddy Against Cyberbullying, is a platform committed to combating the rising issue of cyberbullying. Our goal is to create a safe and supportive online environment where everyone, especially teenagers, feels protected and empowered.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with tools, resources, and community support to fight against cyberbullying and build a more respectful online culture.

Our Approach

We combine education, real-time support, and community engagement to address the emotional and psychological effects of online harassment.

What We Offer:

  • Emotional support through our chatbot, Billy.
  • Comprehensive tips and defense strategies for staying safe online.
  • A community space to connect with others who have faced cyberbullying.
  • Access to up-to-date information on cyberbullying trends and red-alert areas.
